Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Add items to your cart, then click "Place Order". Your order details will be sent to us via WhatsApp for confirmation.

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes! Select "Home Delivery" at checkout and provide your address. Delivery availability and charges will be confirmed by the store.

Are the prices on the website final?

Prices shown are MRP. Actual prices may be lower based on offers, wholesale rates, and quantity discounts. Final price is confirmed before order.

How do I get wholesale pricing?

Click "Request Wholesale Pricing" and send us a WhatsApp message with your requirements. We'll get back with best rates.

Can I pay online?

Payment is handled in-store or on delivery. We accept cash and UPI.
